Despite its name, the Atacama desert is filled with various flowers at one time of the year, which makes it quite a spectacle.

The beautiful thing that we have in Vallenar is that despite being a desert in its interior full of valleys we can find some natural pools to enjoy.

With its constant sunshine, the Atacama climate speeds up the harvest of table grapes and makes the region the first in the country to complete the process that begins in mid-November and ends at the end of March.

In addition to all of the above, if we drive 45 minutes from the city we will arrive at the beach where we can find views and wonderful weather.

Vallenar is a city full of places to discover from the mountains, its rivers and valleys.
