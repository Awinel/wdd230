Site Purpose

The purpose of Bountiful Foods is to attract and retain customers by offering a wide selection of fresh, healthy fruits and providing a convenient and enjoyable experience. The main goal we aim to is: Proving that eat healthy is not expensive as all people think and it can be an enjoyable experience as well as other food can be.

Target Audience

The target audience for our webpage would include:

1. Health-conscious. People who are interested in maintaining a healthy lifestyle and want to incorporate fresh fruits into their diet.

2. Fitness. People who are into fitness and exercise and are looking for healthy, natural sources of energy and nutrition

3. Families. Parents who are interested in providing healthy and nutritious food for their children and incorporating more fruits into their family's diet.

4. Vegans. People who do not eat meat or derivatives of it, they enjoy cooking, experimenting with new recipes, and exploring different types of fruits to add on their diet.

Personas 1
