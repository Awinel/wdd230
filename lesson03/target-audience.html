Target Audience and Scenarios

Audience

The audience we want to focus on is first businesses that would like to reach more people so their business will be noticed and have more good reviews. Then we want to focus on every people who would like to have a trusted and updated web where to find the better place to enjoy as an individual as well as family. Our site will be responsive so no matter where are you trying to access, people of al kind of ages will see how easy will be to get information from businesses and places.

Personas

1.- A new local restaurant of sushi wants to get noticed, they don't know how to reach more people and they would like to promote their discounts for the opening. Using our webpage all people that visit will know where they are and when they open and close.

2.- A family from another city came to spend their vacation in Vallenar, they because of the ignorance (they don't live here), they are not sure where are the best places to eat and visit, they would like to know where to find accurate information about it.

Scenarios

1.- Wants to find where is the best restaurant in the city to go with his family

2.- People are looking for stores that sells shoes at good price

3.- A business wants to know where should be the best place to open a new store where people most often go to shop

4.- Wants to know if there are parties or local events to participate and hang out
